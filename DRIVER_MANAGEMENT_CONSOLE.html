üîÑ How It Works

  1. Driver Joins Group: When a new member joins the Telegram driver group, they are automatically registered
  2. Location Sharing: Drivers share their location in the group to appear on the dashboard
  3. Real-time Updates: Positions update every 5 seconds on the dashboard map
  4. Status Management: Drivers are marked inactive when they leave the group
  5. Security Logging: All activities are logged for audit purposes
